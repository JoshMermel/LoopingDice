Title: Privacy policy for Looping Dice.

For more information, contact joshmermel@gmail.com

Looping Dice does not collect, share, or retain any user data and never will.

Looping Dice does not not request any user permissions.

Looping Dice stores a small amount of game data in the player's google account
so that their completed levels can be transferred from one device to another.
This save file is only accessible to the user.
